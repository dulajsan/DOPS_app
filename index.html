Welcome to DOPS.

DOPS is a web based app which can be used by the traffic policemen to deduct points from the drivers at the traffic offences.This app is based on html,css and javascript.

concept

As the road traffic gets worse, the traffic offence occurrences also increases in an increasing rate.Because of this road accidents also increases. Our main motivation is to make the driver morecareful. The concept of our application, DOPS- Driving Offence Point System, is to grant the driver a number of merit points and deduct a corresponding number of point when he gets caught for an offence.

Impact

  • Automated all the services of department of motor traffic.
  • Driver becomes more careful because the license will be cancelled for a period of 6 months.
  • Bribery rate will go down.
